FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is the "$500 installation" and what does it cover?↓
The $500 covers the one-time setup of your Octo AI agent: onboarding your properties, configuring workflows, connecting any integrations (bank accounts, vendor networks, tenant portals), and customising your agent preferences. After installation, your agent runs 24/7 with zero additional fees if you install before Dec 31, 2026.
Why is the "before Dec 31, 2026" price so much better?↓
Early adopters help us validate the product and provide valuable feedback. In return, you lock in infrastructure costs at our early build cost and never pay a monthly subscription. This is a genuine, time-limited offer — once the window closes on Dec 31, 2026, the standard $500 + $250/month rate applies.
What happens to my agent after Dec 31, 2026 if I installed early?↓
Nothing changes. If you install before the deadline, you keep the early-adopter terms permanently — no monthly subscription, ever. Your agent continues running as normal. The deadline only affects new installations after Jan 1, 2027.
Why not just host the AI myself to save money?↓
You can — but the real cost adds up fast. A Mac Mini starts at $599, a Mac Studio at $1,999+. Add LLM API & token fees ($50–200+/month), electricity and internet ($100+/month), and the time you'll spend as your own IT department. With Octo, you get all of that managed for a one-time $500 installation fee — and zero ongoing infrastructure overhead.
Is my data secure with Octo managing the infrastructure?↓
Yes. Octo uses bank-grade encryption for all financial data, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and never sells your data to third parties. All documents are stored with end-to-end encryption. Our infrastructure is hardened and monitored 24/7 — so you don't have to be.
What cities and markets does Octo support?↓
We're launching in New York City first (Manhattan, then expanding to all five boroughs). California follows in 2027, with nationwide expansion through 2028–2029. Join our waitlist to be notified when we reach your market.
