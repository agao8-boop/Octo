FAQ

Common questions

How does the 60-day free trial work?↓
You get full access to all Octo features for 60 days with no credit card required. After 60 days it auto-converts to the Starter plan at $17/door/month. You can cancel any time before your trial ends with zero charges.
Can I use Octo alongside my current property manager?↓
Yes. Many owners start by letting Octo handle one workflow (like maintenance or rent collection) while they wind down their PM relationship. Octo is designed to take over gradually or all at once — it's your call.
What if I only have 1–2 properties?↓
Octo works great for small landlords too. At $17/door/month you're still saving thousands compared to a traditional property manager. The platform scales from 1 door to 1,000+.
Is my data secure?↓
Yes. Octo uses bank-grade encryption for all financial data, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and never sells your data to third parties. All documents are stored with end-to-end encryption.
What cities and states does Octo support?↓
We're launching in New York City first (Manhattan, then expanding to Brooklyn, Queens, and the outer boroughs). California follows in 2027, with nationwide expansion through 2028–2029. Join our waitlist to be notified when we reach your market.
Do I stay in control, or does Octo make decisions for me?↓
You're always in control. Octo handles routine tasks autonomously (maintenance dispatch, rent reminders, compliance monitoring), but you approve all major decisions — tenant selection, large repairs over your threshold, lease amendments, and more. You decide how much to delegate.
